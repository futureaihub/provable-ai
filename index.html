Zerivox

Provable AI Infrastructure — Turn AI decisions into cryptographically verifiable proof artifacts

Zerivox converts AI loan and risk decisions into signed, tamper-evident proof records that auditors, regulators, and enterprise clients can independently verify.

Problem

Most AI decision systems rely on logs and internal databases to explain outcomes.

These records are mutable and cannot be independently verified. When regulators ask how a specific AI decision was made, teams must reconstruct evidence across logs, models, and internal systems.

This process is slow, fragile, and risky.

Solution

Zerivox enforces deterministic AI decision execution and produces cryptographic proof artifacts.
  • version-locked model + policy
  • deterministic state transitions
  • hash-linked decision ledger
  • ED25519 signed execution records
  • portable proof artifacts
  • independent offline verification

Verification Guarantees

If any part of a decision record is modified, verification fails. Zerivox detects:
  • ledger tampering
  • model version drift
  • policy mismatch
  • illegal state transitions
  • signature forgery
  • cross-environment divergence
